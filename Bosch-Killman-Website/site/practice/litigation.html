Litigation

Civil and commercial litigation in West Michigan's state and federal courts.

How we help

When a dispute can't be resolved across a table, we take it to the courtroom. Our litigators handle civil and commercial matters throughout West Michigan — with the preparation the case deserves and straight answers about its strengths, weaknesses, and costs before you commit to a strategy.

We litigate to win, but we also know that the best result is sometimes the one reached before trial. You'll get honest counsel about settlement at every stage.
