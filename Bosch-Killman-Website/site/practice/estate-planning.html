Practice Area

Estate Planning & Probate

Wills, trusts, and plans that protect West Michigan families and their businesses.

How we help

A good estate plan answers the questions your family would otherwise face at the worst possible time. We prepare wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and patient advocate designations tailored to your family and your assets — including plans that account for a family business or out-of-state property.

When a loved one passes, we also guide personal representatives and families through Michigan probate with clarity about each step and its timeline.

Common matters

  • 01Wills and trusts
  • 02Powers of attorney and patient advocate designations
  • 03Plans for minor children
  • 04Business succession within the estate plan
  • 05Probate administration
