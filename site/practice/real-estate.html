Practice Area

Real Estate

Purchases, sales, leases, and property disputes across West Michigan.

How we help

Real estate is where many of West Michigan's most important investments live. We handle residential and commercial purchases and sales, leases, easements, title questions, and the disputes that arise when property lines, agreements, or expectations don't match.

Our real estate practice works hand-in-hand with our business and estate planning teams — useful when a property sits inside a company or a trust.

Common matters

  • 02Commercial and residential leasing
  • 03Title and boundary disputes
  • 04Easements and land use
  • 05Landlord–tenant matters
