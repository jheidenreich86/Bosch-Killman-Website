Practice Area

Personal Injury

Auto, no-fault, and premises liability matters — with deadlines that matter.

How we help

Injury cases run on deadlines. Michigan law limits how long you have to bring a claim, and no-fault benefits have their own clock. That's why our intake asks for the date of the incident first — so nothing time-sensitive slips.

We handle auto negligence, no-fault, and premises liability matters, and we'll give you a realistic assessment of your claim before you commit to anything.

BKV also serves as defense counsel to insurers in these same categories of claims — see Insurance Defense. Conflicts are checked before any engagement, on either side.
