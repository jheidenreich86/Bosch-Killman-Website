Practice Area

Insurance Defense

No-fault, auto negligence, and premises liability defense for insurers and their insureds — tried, settled, and reported the way claims professionals expect.

Defense counsel built for the file

Insurance defense is the core of our litigation practice. We defend no-fault (PIP) claims, auto negligence suits, and premises liability actions for carriers and their insureds throughout West Michigan's district, circuit, and federal courts — including the fee-schedule and threshold questions that have reshaped Michigan no-fault litigation.

Claims professionals get what they need to manage the file: prompt acknowledgment of assignment, early case assessment with candid exposure analysis, budgets that hold, and reporting on your timeline and format — not ours. Four attorneys — Peter Bosch, Kurt Killman, Julian Heidenreich, and Andrea Koop — anchor the practice, and files are staffed leanly: the attorney you assign is the attorney who works the case.

Common assignments

  • 01No-fault / PIP first-party defense
  • 02Auto negligence and third-party liability defense
  • 03Premises liability defense
  • 04Coverage questions arising from injury claims
  • 05Early case assessment and exposure analysis

The firm also represents individual injury claimants in matters where no conflict exists — see Personal Injury. Conflicts are checked before any engagement on either side.

Our defense group

Carriers, TPAs, and claims professionals: call the office and ask for the desk of the attorney handling your file. If you're establishing a new panel relationship, any member of the group can start that conversation. New assignments are acknowledged the same business day.
