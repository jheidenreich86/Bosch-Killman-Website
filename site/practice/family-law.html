Family Law

Divorce, custody, and support — handled with discretion and steady guidance.

How we help

Family matters are personal, and they deserve counsel that treats them that way. We guide clients through divorce, custody, parenting time, and support with discretion, realistic expectations, and a focus on the outcomes that matter most to you and your children.

If you have concerns about your immediate safety or your children's, tell us in your intake — those matters are reviewed the same day. In an emergency, always call 911 first.
